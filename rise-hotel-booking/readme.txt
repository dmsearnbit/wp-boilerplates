== Description ==

= Rise Hotel Booking for WordPress =

Rise is a free hotel booking plugin for wordpress, **designed by hoteliers, for hoteliers.**

As hoteliers, we have always had problems with the reservations systems, their complexity and difficulty to use.
Now, we have finally created a new hotel reservation system that is **easy to use**, and doesn't cost a dime.
Whether you have a boutique hotel, or a big resort, Rise hotel booking system will help you get reservations easily and directly.

= Main features =

* Show your rooms
* Get bookings
* Integrations for easy credit card payment with Iyzico & Stripe
* Payment options such as offline payment & pay at check-in
* Booking managements
* Pricing plans per room and per period
* Closing rooms for specific dates
* Coupon codes valid for custom dates.
* Custom rates such as included meal, refundable or non-refundable pricings.
* Sending mails to customer and the hotel owner for each booking.

More features coming soon! Interested in a custom feature? Let us know
[Plugin Official Website](https://www.risehotelbooking.com/)

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How much does it cost to use the system? Is there a commission fee? =

The Rise Hotel Booking plugin is completely free and does not require any commissions to be paid per reservation.
